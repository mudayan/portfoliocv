4. Laser Light Gun

In this Computer Aided Design project, I created the design of a laser light gun assembly matching the manufacturer specifications.
The laser light gun assembly has multiple parts which are assembled together to form a single unit.
All the components in the assembly are made out of metal.

The above image is the isometric view of the laser light gun assembly.
The assembly components can be screwed on together with the threads provided on the individual components.

All CAD models of every component in the assembly are made to scale.
All the components of the model can be easily converted into a high-fidelity prototype and a final manufacturing product using SLA or FDM printers.
The components can also be assembled together easily to test the fit and form of the assembly.
When the laser diode and its power source are assembled into the product, functional capability of the product can be tested.
